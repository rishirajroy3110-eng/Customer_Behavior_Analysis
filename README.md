Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis
Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using transactional data from 3,900 purchases across multiple product categories. The objective is to uncover meaningful insights into customer spending patterns, product preferences, subscription behavior, and customer segmentation to support data-driven business decisions.

Objectives
Clean and preprocess raw customer transaction data.
Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify trends and patterns.
Analyze customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and subscription status.
Build interactive dashboards for data visualization.
Generate actionable business recommendations.
Tools & Technologies
Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
PostgreSQL
Power BI
Jupyter Notebook
Gamma AI (Project Presentation)
Key Analysis Areas
Revenue by Gender and Subscription Status
Customer Segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
Product Performance and Customer Ratings
Discount Dependency Analysis
Revenue Distribution by Age Group
Shipping Type Impact on Spending
Subscription Conversion Opportunities
Key Findings
Male customers generated significantly higher revenue than female customers.
Non-subscribers accounted for the majority of total revenue, presenting a strong subscription conversion opportunity.
Loyal customers represented the largest customer segment.
Products such as Gloves and Sandals received the highest average ratings.
Young Adults contributed the highest overall revenue.
Express shipping customers showed slightly higher average purchase values.
Business Recommendations
Increase subscription adoption through exclusive benefits and targeted campaigns.
Implement loyalty programs to retain repeat customers.
Optimize discount strategies for highly discount-dependent products.
Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments.
Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard provides:

Interactive filtering by gender, category, subscription status, and shipping type.
Revenue and customer behavior insights.
Dynamic visualizations for business decision-making.
Project Outcome

This project demonstrates the complete data analytics workflow—from data cleaning and exploratory analysis to SQL querying, dashboard development, and business insight generation—using real-world retail transaction data.
